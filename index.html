About the Artist

JKP Productions

Jordyn Kusumoto-Perez is a character concept illustrator with a BFA in illustration from CSU East Bay. Her work centers on narrative-driven comics in sci-fi, action, mystery, and comedy. She specializes in cartoon-style animal character design, emphasizing expressiveness, visual storytelling, and conceptual development.
